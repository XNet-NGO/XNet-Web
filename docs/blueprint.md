# **App Name**: XNet AI Bridge

## Core Features:

- AI Chat Q&A: An AI-powered chatbot that can answer questions about XNet Inc.'s services, mission, and impact, providing instant support and information to visitors. This feature is intended to function as a tool that enables it to selectively extract, filter, and display contextually appropriate information. It answers in the persona of Joshua S. Doucette.
- Mission and Values Showcase: Display XNet Inc.'s core values and mission in a visually engaging way, highlighting the societal 'Why' behind the company's work.
- Contact Form: A simple form to enable potential clients and partners to reach out to XNet Inc.
- Logo display: The logo that the user uploaded will be shown in the application

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A cool, professional blue (#4682B4) inspired by the logo, evoking trust and intelligence.
- Background color: A very light desaturated blue (#F0F8FF), near-white, to provide a clean and modern backdrop.
- Accent color: A teal color (#008080) to add vibrancy, and subtly echo the coloring used in the company logo, to draw attention to important information, while maintain design elegance.
- Body: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for clarity and readability in longer texts.
- Headline: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, tech-forward look that stands out.
- Use clean, modern icons related to AI and community impact. Consider outline style icons to maintain a sleek appearance.
- Maintain a clean, organized layout with ample white space to ensure easy readability and a professional look. Prioritize key information above the fold.
